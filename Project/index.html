How to Play

Preface: There are two sides in this game. You play as the dog. You as the dog will destroy the eggs hidden by the chickens in a 4x4 field

  1. Press the coordinates you want to target
  2. Press launch button
  3. If the missile launched hits an egg, you get a point.
  4. If you ran out of bullets, you loose.
  5. If you were able to destroy all eggs hidden, you win.
